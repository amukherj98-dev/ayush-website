WHO I AM

I am a Mechanical Engineer pivoting into the world of Project Management. My background is built on the bedrock of physics, thermodynamics, and material science, but my future is focused on scope, schedule, and strategy.

Before this Master’s, I studied Mechanical Engineering at Trinity College Dublin (after earlier Computer Engineering in Hong Kong) and worked as an Analyst at Ernst & Young in Mumbai on investigations and construction projects. That mix taught me how to move between technical detail, client expectations and multi-cultural teams without losing structure.

Currently pursuing my Master's in Project Management, I aim to bridge the gap between complex technical requirements and efficient project delivery. I don't just understand how things are built; I understand how to get them built on time and within budget.
